TwintailLauncher is a **multi-platform launcher** designed to enhance the gaming experience for anime-styled games. It provides **mod support**, **quality-of-life improvements**, and **advanced features** to a variety of games, making it an all-in-one tool for downloading, managing, and launching favorite anime games. The launcher is designed with **flexibility**, **ease of use**, and **customization** in mind.

## Overview
The TwintailLauncher supports a range of popular anime games, including *Genshin Impact*, *Honkai: Star Rail*, *Honkai Impact 3rd*, *Zenless Zone Zero*, *Wuthering Waves*, *Punishing: Gray Raven*, and *AetherGazer*. The launcher aims to provide a seamless gaming experience while ensuring **safety** and **security**.
