# EchoZone

EchoZone is a _location-based_, **anonymous** social media platform that enables users to engage in _real-time_ discussions with their local community. Inspired by [Yik Yak](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yik_Yak), EchoZone allows users to post short messages that are visible only within a defined _geographic radius_, fostering a sense of community and anonymity. This platform is currently in the _early development_ stage, with a planned tech stack that includes **React**, **Node.js**, and **Firebase**.

### Overview

EchoZone is designed to facilitate _local discussions_ and community interaction, providing a platform for users to share their thoughts and ideas without revealing their identities. The platform's _location-based_ feed ensures that users can engage with content that is relevant to their surroundings, making it an ideal tool for _community engagement_ and _social interaction_.

### Features

The core features of EchoZone include:

* **Anonymous posting**, allowing users to share their thoughts without fear of retribution
* _Location-based feed_, which displays posts from users within a defined geographic radius
* **Upvotes and downvotes**, enabling users to interact with and moderate content
* _Real-time updates_, ensuring that users can engage with the latest posts and discussions
* **Content moderation tools**, which help to maintain a safe and respectful community

### Technical Details

The planned tech stack for EchoZone includes:

* **Frontend**: _React_ and _React Native_ for building the user interface
* **Backend**: _Node.js_ and _Express_ for handling server-side logic
* **Realtime Data**: _Firebase_ and _WebSockets_ for enabling real-time updates
* **Maps & Location**: _Mapbox_ for providing location-based services
* **Authentication**: _Anonymous_ and _device-based_ authentication methods

### Status

EchoZone is currently in the _early development_ stage, with a focus on building the core features and tech stack. As the platform continues to evolve, it is expected to provide a unique and engaging experience for users, facilitating _local discussions_ and _community interaction_ in a safe and respectful environment.
